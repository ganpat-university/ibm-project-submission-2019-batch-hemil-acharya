Features


Identify people from image

This feature identifies people from an image who have been missing (if any). It also identifies if any person in that image has ever had any criminal record and seems suspicious.

Search Image

Identify people from Live footage

This feature identifies people who have been missing and people who have ever had any criminal record in real time from live footage. It could also be used for various security purposes in day-to-day life.

Search via livestream

Identify people from Video clip

This feature identifies from a video clip the people who have been missing and people who have ever had any criminal record if they are present in that clip.

About this app


Looking for a person who has gone missing?

Worried that the person last seen around the missing person may have a criminal record?

Or if you want a safety app that identifies any person who comes to your doorstep and informs you beforehand if that person has any past criminal record or is missing?


This app will be able to help you with these issues.
It was made to cater to the above mentioned issues along with these it also provides a few additional features.
